🎮 Tic Tac Toe vs Smart Computer

How to Play:
- You are X, the computer is O.
- Click on any empty square to make your move.
- The computer blocks your winning moves and tries to win too.
- First to align 3 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins!
- If all squares are filled and nobody wins, it’s a draw.
- Your score and the computer’s score will be tracked below.
